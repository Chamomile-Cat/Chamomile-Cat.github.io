My name is Hyebin Seo. I'm an undergraduate student graduating in English Language and Literature at Yonsei University.

Through my experience of private tutoring and developing English textbooks, I became curious how students acquire a second language as well as how students should be assessed. This curiosity drives me to become a well-rounded researcher who can combine information and skills from computer science to linguistics to solve various problems or issues in second language acquisiton and teaching.

My current research interests are:

- Automated Writing Assessment
- Conversation Analysis

In my free time, I study and work on Natural Language Processing projects in Python.
